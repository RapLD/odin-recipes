Broccoli Chicken Casserole

Description

A creamy broccoli and chicken bake that has very rich flavor. The prep time is 15mins and the cook time being 30mins for a total of 45mins. All you need is only 6 ingredients which cost you no more than $15! This recipe could serve 4 people and is very simple steps to make. Which to also add that their is only 3 easy steps to follow. Don't forget to get a baking sheet or have a baking pan and enjoy this delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 pound broccoli florets, cooked
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 cup shredded Chedder cheese
  • 1 cup dry stuffing mix

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. cook chicken on skillet pan until browned and shred or cut into bite size pieces. Meanwhile, steam broccoli until crisp but tender.
  3. In a small bowl mix together the soup and mayonnaise. Set aside. In a 9x13 inch baking dish layer the chicken, broccoli, soup mixture, and cheese. Sprinkle dry stuffing mix over the top and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
